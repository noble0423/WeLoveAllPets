Dogs

DOGS
A Quick “Nature Calls” Moment:

A short and sweet 10-minute outdoor break to stretch your pup's legs, as well as to provide some much-needed potty relief. Ideal for newborn pups and pups in the later stages of life. Oh, and lets not forget the occasional lazy pup. You know who you are!

$17

Doggy Stroll in the Park:

This option is definitely our most popular… A solid 20-minute walk to provide some much-needed potty relief and, of course the best part--exercise and attention. Most certainly ideal for those pups that love to get out and enjoy a little fun in the sun.

$22

Doggy Stride and Strut:

The perfect combination of exercise and relaxation. This 40-minute walk starts by providing a fairly rigorous pace to give your pup a good amount of exercise. Towards the end we slow it down to allow for a bit of a cool down and, of course, to "stop and smell the roses," (or trees and fire hydrants). No judgments--we all have our preferences!

$27

Neighborhood Doggy Safari:

Your pup will have plenty of time to take in the sights, sounds, and of course, smells, as we make our way about town during this 60-minute walk. Okay okay, the neighborhood, but you get what we’re saying. If you have a pup that is just bouncing off the walls with energy, or even a pup that gets extremely anxious and nervous at the mere sight of you going for the door knob, then this is the perfect option. We’ll make sure your pup is all mellowed out and exhausted by the time you get home from work. Good company, stress relief, and exercise! What more could a pup ask for?

$35

Pampered Pet Sleep-Over:

Our Overnight Pet Care option provides you with the peace of mind in knowing that your pup will be cared for around the clock. Your pup will be catered to paw and foo… uh, paw! In fact, we dare say… your pup may not even miss you while you’re away!

$100
