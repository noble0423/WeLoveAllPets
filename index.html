ABOUT US

Hmmm… About Us, huh? Well, simply put we are a group of people whose soul purpose is to love and care for all of the pets of the world!!! Okay okay, granted—we only work out of Houston as of right now. Aaaand, it’s really only Central Houston at that. But just to be clear, we definitely have world domination in our sights as well! Uuuh you know, in the pet care industry that is. So, now that we have that out of the way where were we? Oh yes, simplicity. Let’s just go with what comes to mind:

Here at We Love All Pets, we are dedicated to providing only the best in Elite, Quality Pet Care! Our goal is to ensure that both our Clients, and their pets, have all of their pet care needs met. To that end we strive not only to provide a higher level of pet care, but to develop and build long lasting relationships with both our Clients, and the pets we care for. In this regard, we consider ourselves to be a sort of Personal Pet Concierge. We're not only here just to walk your dog, or feed your cat. We are here to learn about them; all of the little nuances that make up who they are. This way, with each visit, we strengthen the bonds we've created with them. In addition to this, we become our client's personal pet advisor and advocate. Thus we're ultimately creating an environment completely devoid of stress for the pets in our care, and a strong sense of trust coupled with a sound peace-of-mind for our clients. And who wouldn't want that?!

So, whether you're looking for an Over Night Pet Sitter, A Dog Walker, a Cat Sitter, or Heck, even someone to look after your Giant Iguana, we’re here for you, ready to astound you with our next-level love and commitment to Georg… uh your pet! Welcome you to our ever-growing family... WE LOVE ALL PETS!

SERVICES
A Quick “Nature Calls” Moment:

A short and sweet 10-minute outdoor break to stretch your pup's legs, as well as to provide some much needed potty relief. Ideal for new born pups and pups in the later stages of life. Oh, and lets not forget the occasional lazy pup. You know who you are!

$17

Doggy Stroll in the Park:

This option is definitely our most popular… A solid 20-minute walk to provide some much needed potty relief and, of course the best part—Exercise and Attention. Most certainly ideal for those pups that love to get out and enjoy a little fun in the sun.

$22

Doggy Stride and Strut:

The perfect combination of exercise and relaxation. This 40-minute walk starts by providing a fairly rigorous pace to give your pup a good amount of exercise. Then towards the end we slow it down to allow for a bit of a cool down and, of course, to "stop and smell the roses." Or trees and fire hydrants. No judgment. We all have our preferences!

$27

Neighborhood Doggy Safari:

Your pup will have plenty of time to take in the sights, sounds, and smells of course, as we make our way about town during this 60-minute walk. Okay okay, the neighborhood. But you get what we’re saying. If you have a pup that is just bouncing off the walls with energy, or even a pup that gets extremely anxious and nervous at the mere sight of you going for the door knob, then this is the perfect option… We’ll make sure your pup is all mellowed out and exhausted by the time you get home from work. Good company, Stress Relief, and Exercise! What more could a pup ask for?

$35

Pampered Pet Sleep-Over:

Our Over-Night Pet Care option provides you with the peace of mind in knowing that your pup will be cared for round the clock. Your pup will be catered to paw and foo… uh, paw! In fact, we dare say… your pup may not even miss you while you’re away!

Service includes:

  • (2) “Doggie Strolls” (Early Morning and Evening)
  • (2) “Nature Calls” Moments (Mid Day and Bedtime)
  • All Meals prepared according to your instruction
  • Medication (if needed) administered per vet instruction unless otherwise specified.

$100

*** Complimentary Services Included:
  • Watering Plants
